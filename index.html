About COBA Professional Development

The College of Business Administration's Professional Development program exists to empower COBA students with the skills, resources, and confidence to thrive in today's competitive business landscape.

From resume building and interview prep to internship placement and career coaching, we provide comprehensive support tailored to your major and career goals. Our mission is to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world success.
