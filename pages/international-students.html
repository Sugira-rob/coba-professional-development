Understanding H-1B Sponsorship

As an international student at ACU COBA, understanding H-1B sponsorship is crucial for your career planning. Here's everything you need to know.

What is H-1B?

H1B sponsorship is a process whereby a U.S. employer or company hires a foreign professional to work in a specialty occupation that requires a bachelor's degree or equivalent. The employer files a visa petition and a labor condition application with the U.S. immigration and labor departments. If approved, the employee can work for the employer for up to six years, with a possibility of extension in some cases.

Learn more: H1B Sponsorship Guide
