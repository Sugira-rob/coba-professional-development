Management Career Paths

Discover diverse opportunities in business management and leadership

Client Services Manager

Key Responsibilities:
  • Develop and implement customer service procedures
  • Train staff to follow established guidelines
  • Maintain contact with current clients about campaigns and projects
  • Help customers with questions and resolve issues

Sales Representative

Key Responsibilities:
  • Contact new or existing customers to discuss products and services
  • Negotiate contracts and ensure client satisfaction
  • Act as customer service representative for client issues
  • Work with individuals, businesses, and organizations

Project Manager

Key Responsibilities:
  • Organize, plan, direct, and coordinate business projects
  • Ensure projects meet deadline, budget, and company goals
  • Supervise teams and schedule meetings
  • Troubleshoot issues and measure milestones

Office Manager

Key Responsibilities:
  • Organize business operations, procedures, and maintenance
  • Prepare payroll and approve supply requests
  • Manage administrative team and assign duties
  • Oversee office environment and staff management

Human Resources Manager

Key Responsibilities:
  • Oversee employee hiring, firing, and payroll
  • Manage training, development, and governance
  • Research and recruit employees, coordinate interviews
  • Handle payroll, benefits, and incentive programs

Account Executive

Key Responsibilities:
  • Acquire new clients and maintain relationships
  • Serve as point of contact between team and client
  • Strategic planning to help clients reach goals
  • Build and maintain relationships through excellent service
