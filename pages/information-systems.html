Certification Path Recommendations

Start with foundational certifications like CompTIA IT Fundamentals or Google IT Support. Then progress to specialized areas based on your interests: networking (CCNA), security (CEH), or development (W3Schools, Codepath). Many employers value multiple certifications that demonstrate breadth and depth of knowledge.
